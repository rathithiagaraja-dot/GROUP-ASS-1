Indian Languages

India is home to hundreds of languages spoken across different states and communities. Below are five of the most widely spoken and culturally significant languages.

தமிழ் (Tamil)

Tamil Nadu
  • Script: Tamil
  • Speakers: 80+ Million
  • Greeting: வணக்கம் (Vanakkam)
  • Known For: One of the world's oldest living languages.

Basic Words

  • Hello: Vanakkam (வணக்கம்)
  • Thank You: Nandri (நன்றி)
  • Yes: Aam (ஆம்)
  • No:Illai (இல்லை)
  • Goodbye:Poitu Varen (போயிட்டு வரேன்)

മലയാളം (Malayalam)

Kerala
  • Script:Malayalam
  • Speakers:38+ Million
  • Greeting: നമസ്കാരം (Namaskaram)
  • Known For: Rich literature and unique script.

Basic Words

  • Hello:Namaskaram (നമസ്കാരം)
  • Thank You: Nanni (നന്ദി)
  • Yes: Athe (അതെ)
  • No:Alla (അല്ല)
  • Goodbye: Veendum Kaanam (വീണ്ടും കാണാം)

తెలుగు (Telugu)

Andhra Pradesh Telangana
  • Script:Telugu
  • Speakers:95+ Million
  • Greeting:నమస్కారం (Namaskaram)
  • Known For: Popularly called the "Italian of the East".

Basic Words

  • Hello:Namaskaram (నమస్కారం)
  • Thank You : Dhanyavadalu (ధన్యవాదాలు)
  • Yes:Avunu (అవును)
  • No:Kaadu (కాదు)
  • Goodbye:Malli Kaluddam (మళ్లీ కలుద్దాం)

हिन्दी (Hindi)

Northern India
  • Script:Devanagari
  • Speakers: 600+ Million
  • Greeting:नमस्ते (Namaste)
  • Known For:India's most widely spoken language.

Basic Words

  • Hello:Namaste (नमस्ते)
  • Thank You:Dhanyavaad (धन्यवाद)
  • Yes:Haan (हाँ)
  • No:Nahin (नहीं)
  • Goodbye:Alvida (अलविदा)

ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannada)

Karnataka
  • Script:Kannada
  • Speakers:45+ Million
  • Greeting:ನಮಸ್ಕಾರ (Namaskara)
  • Known For:Classical language with over 2,000 years of history.

Basic Words

  • Hello:Namaskara (ನಮಸ್ಕಾರ)
  • Thank You:Dhanyavaadagalu (ಧನ್ಯವಾದಗಳು)
  • Yes:Howdu (ಹೌದು)
  • No:Illa (ಇಲ್ಲ)
  • Goodbye:Matte Sigona (ಮತ್ತೆ ಸಿಗೋಣ)
